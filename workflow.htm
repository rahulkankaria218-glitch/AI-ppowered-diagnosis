Clinical Workflow

A seamless AI-assisted pipeline from scan upload to diagnosis, reporting, and hospital-level insights.

01

Scan Upload

Medical images from X-ray, CT, or MRI devices are securely uploaded to the platform and organized into patient cases.

02

AI Processing & Analysis

Deep learning models analyze scans, detect abnormalities, and generate assistive visual overlays for faster review.

03

Priority Triage

Critical findings are automatically flagged and moved to the priority queue to ensure rapid attention during emergencies.

04

Radiologist Review

Radiologists examine images with AI overlays, compare case history, annotate findings, and validate AI suggestions.

05

Smart Report Generation

The system generates structured preliminary reports which can be edited, finalized, and digitally signed by specialists.

06

Emergency Alerts

Life-threatening cases trigger instant alerts to clinicians and emergency teams for immediate intervention.

07

Hospital Dashboard & Analytics

Administrators access real-time metrics including turnaround time, AI detection accuracy, and workload distribution.
